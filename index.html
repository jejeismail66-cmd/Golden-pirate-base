❓ FAQ & Rules ▼
🪙 What is Gold?
Gold is the main currency and determines your rank on the Leaderboard. Earn it by opening chests, hiring workers, upgrading your ship, and daily claims.
⚙️ What is Silver?
Silver is the key to opening treasure chests. You earn Silver exclusively by completing Tasks. You cannot buy Silver — you must earn it!
📦 How do I open chests?
Go to the Game tab. Each chest costs 1 Silver to open. Tap a chest to select it, then tap the open button. Chests drop Gold and rare Collectibles.
🎁 What are Collectibles?
Collectibles are rare items found inside chests. There are 6 total — Rare (~8%), Epic (~3%), and Legendary (~1%) drop rates. Collecting all 6 unlocks Bonus Gold and Special Prizes!
🦀 What does the Crab Miner do?
Hiring the Crab Miner costs 3 USDC and passively earns you 16 Gold per day automatically. Great for climbing the leaderboard without doing anything!
⛵ What does upgrading my Ship do?
Each ship upgrade earns you more passive Gold daily. Level 2 earns +1 Gold/day, Level 3 earns +2 Gold/day, and so on. Higher levels cost more USDC.
🏆 How does the Leaderboard work?
The leaderboard ranks the top 200 players by Gold earned this season — highest to lowest. Season 1 resets at the end of the countdown. Top players win real prizes!
🔗 Which blockchain is this on?
Golden Pirate Base runs on the Base Network (Chain ID 8453) — a fast, low-fee Ethereum Layer 2 by Coinbase. Connect with MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet.
👥 How does the referral system work?
Share your invite link from the Profile tab. You earn 50% of Silver from any paid claims or ship purchases your friends make. Only real USDC transactions count.
🛡️ Is my wallet safe?
Yes. We never ask for your private key or seed phrase. We only request wallet connection to verify your identity and record your score on Base. You are always in control.
